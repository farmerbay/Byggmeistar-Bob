Lofty

Lofty is a blue mobile crane based of the real-life machine Liebherr LTM 1200-5.1 mobile crane. In the series he is an easily-scared character that has a lack of confidence, which makes him think he can’t do things he can. Lofty is the strongest of Bob’s machines, but this he often forgets. He is a shy and nervous machine but in the end, he always rises and does the things he is supposed to do.
