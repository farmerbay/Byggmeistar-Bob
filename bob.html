Bob

Bob’s catch-phrase is “Can we fix it? Yes, we can!” This says a lot about Bob. He is a builder in Spring City, where he thinks no job is to small or to big. He is very dedicated and the leader of the team. A characteristic personality trait used to describe Bob is optimistic. He always believes he will find a solution, and he does. Often with the fabulous help from his helpers.
